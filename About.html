About Intellect

Intellect is an innovative personal AI assistant designed to streamline your daily tasks and enhance productivity. Our mission is to provide users with a seamless and intelligent way to manage their digital lives. Intellect leverages cutting-edge technology in natural language processing and machine learning to deliver a personalized and intuitive user experience.

Currently, in its alpha phase, Intellect i0 is available exclusively for Inter-IIT students, offering real-time access to event updates, leaderboards, and more. This initial release helps us refine our platform and gather valuable user feedback.

Looking ahead, Intellect i1 will mark our first public release, available to beta users on the Google Play Store. This version will feature enhanced functionalities, including Gmail processing, automated follow-ups, model personalization, and custom recurring task automation based on personal data.

Join us on our journey to revolutionize the way people interact with technology. Your feedback is crucial in shaping the future of Intellect.
